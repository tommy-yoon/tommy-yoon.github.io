A problem solving story

The Built-in Methods part was the most challenging to me. I got stuck again and again at almost every step. So, I used the below problem solving methods.

  • Reading error messages: this is the most important and the first thing to do analyse the problem. in many cases, problem can be quickly solved by just seeing a line of error message.
  • Pseudocode: either writing a quick note about the logic of the function required or just imagning it in my head.
  • Console.logging: if error messages doesn't show the details problem, I should add console loggings to find out the exactly right place causing the issue.
  • Googling: because there are many things I don't know about JavaScript such as syntax, functions and typical patterns of problem solving, Googling is unavoidable to get information. Through Googling, I visited MDN and StackOverflow site a lot. Sometimes, I felt a bit guilty as I could find the exact solution on StackOverflow and could solve my problem by just copy the codes from there.

For instance, I couldn't understand the different usages of the built-in methods of arrays. I just googled and found a good explanation from a Stack Overflow user, Pointy as below. Now I have a good understanding about it. As long as I live as a developer, probably, I will keep visiting the site to get help.
