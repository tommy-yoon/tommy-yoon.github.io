Learning Plan

Obstacles found!

As it is online, I have to manage everything myself. Nobody tells me what to do and when to do that. So, I easily get lazy.

Solution?

1. I need to have a time table which tells when to focus on the study. So, I will have two focus times. In the morning from 10AM to 1PM for 3 hours. Another one is 3PM to 6PM for 3 hours. During focus times, I won't be distracted by anything including my mobile phones, meaningless web surfing and etc.

2. No more catching up but do everything on time and try to do study extended materials to expand my experience as well.

And these are my general plan.

  • Ultimate goal - Becoming a web developer and make a living and live happily forever.
  • Expectation of myself in Bootcamp - being familiar with web development skills and have tons of practice with them.
  • Learning path - As I have some experience as back-end programming, I'b probably better to focus on JavaScript parts so I will be more specialized in dynamic parts of web development field.
  • Strength and limitations - Lack of self control(laziness) is my issue but once I get interested in a topic, I can focus on that topic and study hard. After foundation, I became confident as I could overcome my laziness so I am sure everything will be okay.
  • Non-tech skill - I need to get used to New Zealand cluture and improve my English skill
  • Commitment - I will follow the above focus times(morning and afternoon) then it will be okay
