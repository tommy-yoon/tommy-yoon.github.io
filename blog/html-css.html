What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

  • Padding is the gap between the content and the border. It is transparent area so you cannot paint it.
  • Border exists between padding and margin. It can have colour and various type such as a solid line and a dotted line.
  • Margin is the space outside the border of an element. It is transparent area so you cannot paint it.
