Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations.

My personality from the Myers Briggs (16 personalities) test is ISTP-T( https://www.16personalities.com/istp-personality). I am a natural problem-solver. I find a problem and think why it happened by analysing symptoms of the problem. Then guess paths to get to a solution and simulate in head. Lastly, plan the actions to take and do it.

Evaluate your strengths and limitations in terms of your learning and career development

As it is well described in the website about the ISTP-T type personality, I am a born problem-solver. I am curious, like practical ways and love hands-on experiences. I get easily interested in a new topic but get bored easily as well. So, I speed up sometimes but, like now, sometime behind schedule. It is hard to keep focusing on the same topic as I can get bored quickly and hard to keep working at the same pace all the way. However, even though ups and downs, my productivity seems quite good in a long run as when I run I run fast as well. I just need more risk and schedule management as it is a bit stressful when I am behind. So, in my study time, I need to promise myself that I will study everyday equal time rather than being behind and then hurry to catch up.
